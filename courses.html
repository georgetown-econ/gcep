Training courses

Our training courses run over 1 or 2 days in a state-of-the-art econometrics laboratory at Georgetown University. They are designed to be of particular benefit to economists and social scientists in the public and private sectors wanting to know how to use econometric methods and a variety of data to inform policy making.

If you have any queries, please contact events team.
