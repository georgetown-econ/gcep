About us

The Georgetown Center of Econometric Practice (GCEP) is an initiative of the Georgetown Center of Economic Research located in the Economics Department at Georgetown University.

Our mission is to improve the quality of economic policy debate by increasing the level and practice of econometric knowledge in the DC economic research and policy communities. To do so we are partnering with the Center of Microdata Methods and Practice (CEMMAP) to provide a number of activities. These will range from training courses covering the fundamentals of econometric analysis to masterclasses taught by experts working on the frontier of econometric research.
