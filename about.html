About us

The Georgetown Center of Econometric Practice (GCEP) is an initiative of the Georgetown Center of Economic Research located in the Department of Economics at Georgetown University.

The primary objective of GCEP is to improve the quality of the economic debate by increasing the level of econometric awareness in the DC community. To this purpose, GCEP is partnering with the Center for Microdata Methods and Practice (CeMMAP) in London to provide a number of activities. These range from training courses covering the fundamentals of econometric analysis to masterclasses taught by leading experts working on the frontier of econometric research.
