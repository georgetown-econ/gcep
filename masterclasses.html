Each masterclass offers the opportunity to hear a notable contributor to a body of knowledge give an authoritative account of a field of theoretical or applied research. A mix of PhD students, academic and professional researchers, and business analysts from the USA and worldwide attend.

The price for each of the masterclasses is as follows: Students from Higher Education Institutions $500; Government and non profit organization employees $950; All others $1750.  Note we will assign a limited number of places for students, and we will offer a discounted price for organizations which send multiple participants.
