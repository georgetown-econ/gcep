GCEP Mission

Our mission is to contribute to the economic policy debate by increasing the quality of the econometric knowledge and practice in the DC economic research and policy communities. To do so we are partnering with the Center of Microdata Methods and Practice (CeMMAP) to provide a number of activities ranging from training courses to seminars and masterclasses.
